Content

What should you put on your website, especially the landing page?

Always check examples:

  • Emily Zabor
  • Jeff Leek
  • Hadley Wickham
  • Jenna Krall
  • Lizzy Gibson
  • Marianthi

Some takeaways:

  • Name / title / affiliation
  • Brief intro / bio
  • Pointers to projects / papers
  • Contact info

Don’t forget to steal things! That’s how websites are made. For example, I like the picture structure Emily and Lizzy used, and stole that to embed a picture of Ted.

Most of the previous examples were structured in roughly the same way, but you can go all Web 2.0 if you want:

  • Emi Tanaka and her showcase
  • Alison Hill

They have blogdown references here and here
